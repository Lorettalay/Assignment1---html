Ivar the Boneless

Viking Leader | Strategist | Fearsome Warrior

Ivar the Boneless was a Legendary Viking chieftan. He was the son of a Danish king named Ragnarr Lothbrok. Ivar got the nickname "the Boneless" because he was unable to walk and he had a skeletal condition that affected his bones. He became famous for his exploits on the British Isles, and he led "The Great Heathen Army" to conquer parts of England with his brothers.

Skills

  • Leadership and Command
  • Psychological Warfare
  • Cunning and Deception
  • Navigation and Raiding Coordination
  • Intimidation and Fear Tactics
  • Adaptability and Resilience
